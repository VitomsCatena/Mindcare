Free Tools & Techniques

Simple, free exercises and informational support you can use right now.

Guided Breathing: Box Breathing (4x4)

This technique is a powerful way to regulate your nervous system. Try to focus only on the count:

  1. **Inhale** slowly for a count of four (4).
  2. **Hold** your breath for a count of four (4).
  3. **Exhale** slowly for a count of four (4).
  4. **Hold** your breath (empty lungs) for a count of four (4).

Repeat the cycle five times, or until you feel a sense of calm return.

Grounding Technique: 5-4-3-2-1

Use this method when you feel overwhelmed or lost in thought. It helps bring your focus back to the present moment using your five senses.

  • **5** - Name five things you can **SEE**.
  • **4** - Name four things you can **TOUCH**.
  • **3** - Name three things you can **HEAR**.
  • **2** - Name two things you can **SMELL**.
  • **1** - Name one thing you can **TASTE**.
